## Question

Can syscall histograms explain p99 request latency without adding application-level tracing?

## Plan

Attach probes to read, write, epoll, futex, and network-related tracepoints. Compare histograms with application latency under controlled load.

## Constraints

The probe must avoid per-event logging on hot paths and should emit aggregated histograms.
