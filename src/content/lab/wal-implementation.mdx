## Question

What is the smallest WAL format that can survive partial writes and still recover quickly?

## Sketch

Each record stores length, checksum, payload, and commit marker. Segment recovery scans until the first invalid record and truncates from that point.

## Measurements

The useful metrics are append latency, recovery time, segment size, checksum cost, and fsync policy impact.
